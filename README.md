# PrimeAuto Ltd Business Intelligence Dashboard

## Overview
This project presents a Business Intelligence (BI) and Data Analytics solution developed for **PrimeAuto Ltd**, a mid-sized automotive retailer operating across multiple regions and vehicle brands.  

The project focuses on identifying performance imbalances through dashboard-driven analytics and proposing data-driven strategies to improve decision-making, customer segmentation, and operational efficiency.

The dashboard was developed using **Power BI** and supported by key business intelligence and analytics frameworks.

---

# Project Objectives

The project aims to:

- Analyse sales data to identify patterns in:
  - Gender-based revenue distribution
  - Regional sales performance
  - Product preferences

- Apply relevant theoretical frameworks including:
  - Data Analytics Lifecycle
  - SERVQUAL Model
  - Balanced Scorecard
  - Customer Segmentation Theory

- Design an interactive BI dashboard with:
  - KPI monitoring
  - Revenue tracking
  - Customer segmentation
  - Product-level analytics

- Provide recommendations for:
  - Predictive analytics
  - Real-time data integration
  - CRM integration
  - Improved strategic decision-making

# Problem Statement

Although PrimeAuto Ltd reports strong headline performance indicators, dashboard analysis reveals significant structural imbalances.

## Key Issues Identified

### Gender Imbalance
- Male customers contribute approximately:
  - **£16bn (80.56%)**
- Female customers contribute:
  - **£4bn (19.44%)**

This suggests that marketing and sales strategies are disproportionately focused on male customers, leaving a major female customer segment underutilised.

### Regional Imbalance
Some regions significantly outperform others:

| Region | Revenue |
|---|---|
| Austin | ~£3.4bn |
| Janesville | ~£3.2bn |
| Lower-performing regions | ~£2.5–£2.6bn |

This highlights:
- Uneven market penetration
- Weak resource allocation
- Limited regional optimisation

# Key Findings

## Revenue by Gender
| Gender | Revenue | Percentage |
|---|---|---|
| Male | £16bn | 80.56% |
| Female | £4bn | 19.44% |

### Insight
PrimeAuto Ltd is heavily dependent on a single customer segment, increasing strategic risk and limiting growth opportunities.

---

## Revenue by Transmission
| Transmission | Revenue | Percentage |
|---|---|---|
| Automatic | £11bn | 52% |
| Manual | £9bn | 48% |

### Insight
There is a moderate preference for automatic vehicles, influencing inventory planning decisions.

---

## Revenue by Engine Type
| Engine Type | Revenue |
|---|---|
| DOHC | £10.5bn |
| OHC | £9.5bn |

### Insight
Customers show stronger demand for advanced engine technology.

---

## Revenue by Body Style
### Highest Performing
- SUVs
- Hatchbacks

### Lowest Performing
- Hardtops

### Insight
Customer demand strongly favours SUVs, informing marketing and inventory strategies.
